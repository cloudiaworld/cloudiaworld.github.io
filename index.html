CLOUDia is a low-cost, high resolution network for monitoring physical and environmental variables

Cities and infrastructure

We live in an era of unprecedented urbanization, climate change, and population growth. Cities face a great many challenges regarding energy supply, environmental concerns, and, ultimately, the well-being of their citizens.

Agriculture

Agriculture faces the major challenge of adapting to climate change and guaranteeing a sustainable food supply. Rural Advisory Services and farmers urgently require timely, hyperlocal information, tailored for their particular needs.

Energy

The ongoing and future transformations affecting life in cities and rural areas are, doubtless, driven by a sustainable and secure energy supply. Opportune, high resolution information is a valuable input in the design, operation and optimization of energy systems.

CLOUDia provides a better way of understanding our cities, our weather, our energy, and our food

Our solution comprises the hardware nodes for collecting distributed measurements, the cloud platform for managing data, and a cloud-based software to generate useful insights and information.
