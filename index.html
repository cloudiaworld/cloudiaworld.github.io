CLOUDia provides a better way of understanding our cities, our weather, our energy, and our food

The solution comprises the hardware nodes for collecting distributed measurements, and the cloud software for aggregating the measurements, making sense of the data, and generating useful information and knowledge. This knowledge is ultimately used for better understanding and optimizing systems in different fields: agriculture, smart, responsive cities, energy supply.

The built-in communication technologies (WiFi, LoRa, BT, GSM) provide a great connectivity and versatility.
